Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2025  |  Effective: June 16, 2025

Aarvex Global ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with us through WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram Direct Messages, or our other communication channels.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through our messaging channels, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and messaging platform user ID
  • Messages and inquiries you send to us
  • Platform metadata (message ID, timestamp) for deduplication purposes
  • Your contact details if voluntarily provided (phone number, email)

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government IDs, or health data through our messaging channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about our import/export products and services
  • Provide customer support and after-sales assistance
  • Send you relevant business information you have requested
  • Improve the quality of our automated responses
⚠️ We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Automated Messaging (AI Bot)

We use an AI-powered automated response system to reply to your messages on WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram Direct. This system:

  • Processes your message text to generate a relevant response
  • Does not store your messages beyond what is necessary for response generation
  • Uses OpenRouter AI (third-party AI provider) to generate responses — your message text is transmitted to this service for processing
  • Implements a cooldown mechanism to prevent duplicate responses

If you do not wish to interact with our automated system, you may request to speak with a human representative at any time.

4. Meta Platform Data

Our app integrates with Meta's platforms (WhatsApp Business API, Facebook Messenger, Instagram). In accordance with Meta's Platform Terms:

  • We only access messaging data necessary to respond to your inquiries
  • We do not use Meta platform data for advertising or targeting purposes
  • We comply with Meta's Platform Policy and Developer Policies
  • Message IDs are stored temporarily in our database (AWS DynamoDB) solely to prevent duplicate processing

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on AWS (Amazon Web Services) infrastructure located in the Asia Pacific region. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls and authentication for all system components
  • Regular security reviews of our systems

Message records are retained for a maximum of 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data from our systems
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out — Stop receiving automated messages by sending "STOP" or contacting us directly

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp) — Messaging platform infrastructure
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services) — Cloud hosting and database
  • OpenRouter AI — AI response generation

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our messaging services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
